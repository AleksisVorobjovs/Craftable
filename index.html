Inspired by Wordle and other variations of Wordle.

Guess the Minecraft crafting recipe.

Example

Green means the block is in the correct place.

Yellow means the block is in the recipe, but can't be at the placed spot.

Since the recipe can be placed anywhere in the table, there can be multiple correct answers. As well as a fully green table, while not being the correct.

Both example 3 and 4 are correct.
